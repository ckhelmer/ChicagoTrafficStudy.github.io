Region Map: Drunk Driving

This map illustrates the incidence of drunk driving by region. Regions with darker colors have a higher rate of drivers who were involved in an accident where they later tested positive for a BAC of above .08.

This type of visualization is called a choropleth map. It was created using the Google Maps API.

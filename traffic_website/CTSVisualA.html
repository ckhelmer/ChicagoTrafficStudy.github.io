Heatmap with Traffic Cameras Marked

This map tracks the incidence of accidents in the city of Chicago. During our study, we observed that areas that had more traffic had more accidents. This correlated roughly with traffic levels on a daily basis as well.

There are two types of traffic cameras employed by the city of Chicago: red light cameras and speed cameras. They are marked with different colors in the graphic. In the city of Chicago, speed cameras are found only in areas adjacent to a school or a park, and are intended to decrease the fatality rate of accidents, specifically those involving children.
